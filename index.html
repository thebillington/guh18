Welcome to 90s Steam

Prepare your tastebuds for a feast. 90s steam is a website dedicated to ensuring you can re-live your childhood games, such as the version of Space Invaders below.

Trump Space Invaders

90s Steam was built for the Great Uni Hack 2018 in Manchester for the challenge put forward by American Express.

The challenge was to take a modern day website and give it a retro, 90s twist. Naturally, with my inkling for games, I had to build a Steam client that the 90s would be proud of, and here it is.

If you want to find out more about me and who I am, check out the about page.

If you want to check out some of my games, including some that I've built before this hackathon, check out the games page.

To see my open source projects check out my github. All of the code I produce at game jams or hackathons, including this code from the Great Uni Hack, is on there.
